Learn More

Crafted to diminish the visible signs of ageing, this supercharged serum is infused with high concentrations of potent actives - all targeting the regeneration of skin cells and replenishing nourishment, the Platinum Ageless Serum is quickly absorbed deep into the skin’s layers. Boosting collagen production, ensuring and locking in needed. Enriched with vitamin C and E, the skin is brightened, and restored to its utmost radiance - for a timeless and youthful look.

Key Benefits

  • A primer step to achieving and retaining an ageless inner brilliance.
  • Anti-Oxidant filled to fight and reverse the damage caused by free radicals.
  • Boosts and locks in moisture to soothe and further soften the skin.
  • Speeds up the natural renewal process for visibly rejuvenated skin, and the elimination of unwanted texture and pigmentation.

How to Use

STEP 1

Gently warm a small amount of the serum with the ends of your fingers.

STEP 2

Lightly tap over the face and neck. Careful not to apply too much pressure.

STEP 3

Slowly sweep upwards until the serum has been entirely absorbed and follow with the rest of your ritual.
